Project Overview

The Blood Donation Management System is designed to facilitate blood donations by connecting donors with hospitals and patients in need. It ensures efficient blood tracking, donor management, and role-based authentication.



Donor Management

Register and manage blood donors, track their donation history, and ensure eligibility.

Blood Availability

Hospitals can check real-time blood availability and request urgent donations.

Patient Requests

Patients in need of blood can submit requests and receive timely assistance.
