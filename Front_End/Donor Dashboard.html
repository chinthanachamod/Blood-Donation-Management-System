Blood Donation Guidelines
  • Eligibility: Donors must be in good health, at least 18 years old, and weigh over 50kg.
  • Before Donation: Eat a healthy meal, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol 24 hours before donation.
  • During Donation: Remain calm, follow staff instructions, and report any discomfort.
  • After Donation: Drink plenty of fluids, avoid heavy exercise for 24 hours, and monitor for any side effects.
  • Donation Frequency: Males can donate every 3 months, females every 4 months.
